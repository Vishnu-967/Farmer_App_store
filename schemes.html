Seasonal Priority
🌾

PM Fasal Bima Yojana (Crop Insurance)

Financial Protection against Crop Loss

Eligibility Criteria

All farmers including sharecroppers and tenant farmers growing notified crops.

Required Documents

Aadhaar, Bank Passbook, Land records/Rent agreement, Sowing certificate.

⏳ Last date for Kharif: July 31, 2026.
Apply Online (PMFBY)

PM Krishi Sinchayee (Irrigation)

Eligibility Criteria

Farmers with valid land ownership and access to a water source.

Aadhaar, Land records, Quotation from registered dealer, Water source proof.

📅 Apply before Monsoon push: July 31, 2026.
